About Seventeen

SEVENTEEN is a k-pop boy group with 13 members. The names are S.coups, Jeonhan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonoo, Woozi, The8, Mingyu, DK, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino. They had their debut on May 26, 2015, and have been together since. Mostly all of the members have some songs of their own, and 3 of the members have made some songs together. BSS is made up of Hoshi, DK, and Seungkwan and they have made songs all together rather than by their selves. They have a fandom name, and it's carat.
