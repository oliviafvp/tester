About ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN is k-pop boy group with 7 members. In the group there's Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki. 3 of the members however, are not korean, Jay is half american and half korean, Jake, is australian, and Ni-ki is japanese. This group debuted on November 30, 2020. There fandom name is engene. They are one of the only groups without a rapper.
